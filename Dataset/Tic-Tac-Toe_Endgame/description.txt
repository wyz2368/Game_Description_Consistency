Consider a game played between two players, X and O, who alternate turns marking the empty spaces of a 3x3 grid with their respective marks ("x" or "o").

The player who places three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
The winner receives a payoff of 1, and the loser receives a payoff of -1.
If the grid is completely filled without a winner, the game is a draw and both players receive a payoff of 0.

Assume the game begins from the following board state, where "e" represents an empty space:

e | x | e
x | e | o
x | e | o

Player O is the next to move.
