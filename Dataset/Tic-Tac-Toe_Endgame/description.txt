Consider a game played between two players, X and O, who alternate turns on a 3x3 grid, marking empty spaces with "x" and "o" respectively.

The game ends immediately when a player places three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row; that player wins the game.
The winner receives a payoff of 1, and the loser receives a payoff of -1.
If the grid is completely filled without a winner, the game is a draw and both players receive a payoff of 0.

Assume the game begins from the following board state, where "e" represents an empty space and the outer numbers represent the coordinate axes:

    0   1   2
0 | e | x | e |
1 | x | e | o |
2 | x | e | o |

Player O is the next to move.
