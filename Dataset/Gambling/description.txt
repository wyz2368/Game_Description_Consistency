A gambler decides whether to enter a casino.
If he stays out, both the gambler and the casino receive get 0.
If he enters, he plays Matching Pennies against the dealer: each simultaneously chooses Heads or Tails.
If the choices match, the gambler wins and the dealer loses; otherwise, the gambler loses and the dealer wins. 
In either case, the winner's gain equals the loser's loss, which equals to 1.
