Consider a game played between two players, Alice and Bob, that begins with three piles containing 2, 2, and 1 stones, respectively.
Alice makes the first move, and the players alternate turns.

On their turn, the acting player removes either one or two stones from a single pile.

The player who removes the final stone from the board wins the game.
The winner receives a payoff of 1, and the loser receives a payoff of -1.
